🔍 What They Do

Antavo is an enterprise-grade loyalty cloud platform. They provide the underlying technology for large brands (retail, fashion, beauty, lifestyle) to build, manage, and scale complex, omnichannel customer loyalty and reward programs.

Rather than just basic "points-for-purchase," Antavo specializes in experiential loyalty, gamification, and recognizing customer behaviors outside of pure transactions (e.g., recycling, writing reviews, active lifestyle).

🚀 Client Poaching Strategies

The Pain Point: Antavo's API-first headless nature is great, but it requires heavy developer resources or expensive SI (System Integrator) partners to build the front-end and maintain connections.

Our Incentive/Offer:

  • Offer "Out-of-the-box" native integrations that require zero dev work (e.g., 1-click Shopify/Salesforce Commerce Cloud integration).
  • Waive implementation fees if they sign a 2-year contract.
  • Messaging: "Stop paying agencies to manage your loyalty program. Switch to us and redirect your IT budget to actual marketing."

The Pain Point: Enterprise solutions take 6-12 months to deploy. Marketers get impatient.

Our Incentive/Offer:

  • Offer a "Fast-Track Migration" guarantee: Live in 60 days or the first 3 months are free.
  • Provide a dedicated, free data migration team to pull their historical member data from Antavo securely via API.
  • Messaging: "Don't wait a year for an update. We will migrate your millions of members seamlessly, with zero downtime for your customers."

The Pain Point: Pricing based on API calls can balloon unexpectedly during peak holiday seasons (Black Friday).

Our Incentive/Offer:

  • Offer a flat-rate tier based on active members, completely removing API transaction limits.
  • Offer an "ROI Guarantee" — if they don't see a 15% lift in repeat purchase rate within 12 months, give them a heavy discount on renewal.
  • Messaging: "Punished for your own success? Our flat-rate model means you never pay extra just because you had a successful marketing campaign."
